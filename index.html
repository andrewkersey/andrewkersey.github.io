Design Process
Collect
Embarking on new project can be an overwhelming task. The first step in my process involves sitting down with the client and collecting any useful information and imagary to get the creative ball rolling.
Collaberate
Upon seeing the clients creative vision I can begin to collaberate with them. This allows us to bounce ideas off of one another creating a visual platform for me to start to put the pixels together.
Create
This is the part of the process where the task at hand becomes reality. After seeing the clients vision and and racking the right side of my brain, I am now ready to begin to make the vision into a reality.
